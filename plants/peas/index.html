How to Grow Peas

A simple guide for sweet, productive garden peas

About peas

Peas (Pisum sativum) are cool-season vegetables that are easy to grow and produce delicious pods. They climb using tendrils and benefit from support, even for shorter varieties. Peas are perfect for beginners and great for early-season harvests.

Choosing the right site

  • Light: Full sun to light shade.
  • Soil: Moist, fertile, well-drained soil.
  • Temperature: Peas prefer cool weather — ideal for spring sowing.
Tip: Add compost before planting, but avoid heavy nitrogen fertilisers — peas fix their own nitrogen.

Choosing varieties

  • Shelling peas: Classic peas removed from pods.
  • Snow peas: Flat, edible pods.
  • Mangetout: Sweet, crunchy pods eaten whole.
  • Dwarf peas: Compact, good for pots.
  • Climbing peas: Tall, high-yielding, need strong support.

Sowing pea seeds

When to sow

  • Outdoors: Early spring to early summer.
  • Autumn: Hardy varieties can be sown for overwintering in mild areas.

How to sow

  1. Make a shallow trench about 5 cm deep.
  2. Sow seeds 5–7 cm apart.
  3. Space rows 45–60 cm apart.
  4. Cover with soil and water gently.
  5. Provide support immediately after sowing.

Looking after peas

Watering

Keep soil evenly moist, especially during flowering and pod formation.

Feeding

Peas rarely need feeding — compost at planting time is usually enough.

Support

  • Use netting, pea sticks, or mesh.
  • Climbing varieties need tall supports.

Common problems

Pigeons

Protect young plants with netting.

Mildew

Occurs in dry weather — water regularly and mulch soil.

Slugs and snails

Protect seedlings, especially early in the season.

Harvesting peas

  • Shelling peas: Pick when pods are plump.
  • Snow peas: Harvest when pods are flat and tender.
  • Mangetout: Pick young for best sweetness.

Regular picking encourages more pods.

Quick reference

  • Type: Cool-season vegetable.
  • Sowing depth: 5 cm.
  • Spacing: 5–7 cm.
  • Support: Recommended for all types.
  • Harvest: Late spring to summer.
